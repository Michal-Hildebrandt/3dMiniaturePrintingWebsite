About us

We are focused on product quality

The figures can be printed on the latest printer model in SLA technology in 4K resolution, which ensures very high detail of the model. We clean the figures with an ultrasonic cleaner and harden them with professional UV lamps.

Don't worry about shipping !

Every miniature is protected by bubble wrap and additional foil, which guarantee no damage during tranpost. If your order value exceed 50 $, shipping is free !

We can change scale your ordered miniature

If your provided STL model allows it - we can rescale your print from 28 mm to over 150 mm !
